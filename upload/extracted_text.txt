 CHAPTER 01



 Executive Summary
 RZR Global — the AI-driven advertising platform formerly known as Aarki — is scaling across
 eight global offices (San Francisco, New York, London, Tel Aviv, Bangalore, Beijing, Manila, and
 Seoul), processing over 5 million mobile ad requests per second from 10 billion+ devices. As the
 business expands, two structural gaps have surfaced that are directly impacting performance,
 manager effectiveness, and cultural cohesion: the absence of a structured onboarding
 experience and the lack of any formal manager or leadership capability development. This plan is
 the operating blueprint for the Senior L&D; Lead role, designed to close those gaps and build the
 learning infrastructure that defines how RZR develops its people through Year 1 and into Year 2.

 The mandate is sequenced around two urgent priorities. First, within 30 days, revamp, rebuild, and
 relaunch RZR Academy as a globally consistent onboarding experience across all markets. Second, within
 90 days, design and launch a structured Manager Capability Development program for first-time and new
 managers globally. From there, the role expands into a full-stack L&D; calendar, leadership development,
 function-specific training, and a measurement framework built on the Kirkpatrick L1–L3 model. The plan is
 explicitly AI-native: every workflow considers AI augmentation for content generation, personalization,
 translation, simulation, and analytics.

 CHAPTER 03



 Strategic Approach & Guiding Principles
 The L&D; function at RZR will be built on five operating principles. These are not slogans — they translate
 into concrete design decisions across every workstream, from how content is authored to how programs
 are measured and how the team is structured. The principles are deliberately aligned with RZR's own
 cultural narrative of intelligence making impact and with the builder mindset the role demands.


 The Five Principles

                AI-Native by Default
                Every workflow starts by asking: how does AI augment this? Content drafting, personalization,

   P1           translation, simulation, analytics — AI is the operating layer, not a feature.

                BEHAVIOR → LLM-assisted content (3–5x throughput), AI persona role-play for manager training,
                AI-surfaced cohort insights.




Confidential · For Discussion                                                                                        06
    RZR Senior L&D Lead Implementation Plan




                Builder Mindset

  P2
                Design from zero. No legacy constraints, no inherited templates. Every artifact is built to be measured,
                iterated, and either scaled or retired within 2 cycles.

                BEHAVIOR → MVP-first launches; 90-day assessment on every cohort; explicit retire/iterate decisions.




                Measurement-Obsessed
                No program ships without a Kirkpatrick L1–L3 plan. Decisions to continue, improve, or retire are made

   P3           on data, not opinion. Quarterly readout to ELT is non-negotiable.

                BEHAVIOR → MEI v1 in 90 days; L1 surveys on every session; L2 knowledge checks; L3 30/60/90
                follow-ups.




                Globally Consistent, Locally Adapted

  P4
                One program architecture across all markets. Regional variants only where cultural, legal, or language
                considerations demand. Avoid the trap of 8 separate onboarding programs.

                BEHAVIOR → Single Academy architecture with regional overlay; local-language tracks by cohort.




                Manager as Multiplier

   P5
                Manager capability is the highest-leverage L&D; investment. A great manager improves the
                performance of 6–10 ICs; a poor one destroys it. Manager dev gets disproportionate resourcing.

                BEHAVIOR → FTM program, Managers Learning Series, MEI driving manager promotion decisions.



 These principles also serve as the design review criteria for every program. Before any initiative moves
 from design to launch, it must pass a five-question check: Is AI used where it could augment? Is the
 measurement plan defined? Is the global architecture preserved with regional variants only where
 necessary? Does this build manager capability? Is there a 90-day effectiveness assessment built in?
 Programs that cannot answer yes to all five are sent back to design.

 Workstream Deep-Dives
 Workstream 1 · RZR Academy — Global Onboarding
 RZR Academy is the front door of the company. The revamp will produce a modular architecture with three
 layers. Delivery blends async self-paced modules (Notion + LMS), live cohort sessions (regional), and
 structured manager check-ins. Automation: new-hire auto-enrollment triggers from BambooHR,
 day-1/7/30/60/90 milestone nudges via Notion or LMS workflow. Every cohort gets a 90-day effectiveness
 assessment measuring knowledge retention (L2), role readiness (manager-rated, L3), and cultural
 alignment (self-report + manager observation).
